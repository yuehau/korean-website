🖼️ HOW TO ADD YOUR IMAGES
===========================

This folder is where photos and food pictures go.

📸 MEMBER PHOTOS (for the About page)
--------------------------------------

Save your member photos with these EXACT filenames:

  member1.png   (yellow card 一)
  member2.png   (mint card 二)
  member3.png   (coral card 三)
  member4.png   (purple card 四)
  member5.png   (blue card 五)

The photo will automatically appear over the colored square — no HTML edit needed.

⭐ FOR THE BEST "ATLASSIAN-STYLE" CUT-OUT LOOK:
Remove the background from your photo first so the person stands cleanly on the
colored square. Free tools that do this in 1 click:

  • remove.bg          → https://www.remove.bg
  • photoroom.com      → https://www.photoroom.com
  • Microsoft Designer → https://designer.microsoft.com

Then save the result as PNG (preserves transparency). Drop it here as
member1.png / member2.png / etc.

If you don't remove the background, .png or .jpg still works — the photo will
fill the colored square instead of looking cut-out.

🍱 FOOD PHOTOS (for the Vocabulary reference grid)

Download from Unsplash and save as:

  hero.jpg          → background for home page hero
  bibimbap.jpg
  bulgogi.jpg
  sandwich.jpg
  ramen.jpg
  kimchi.jpg
  naengmyeon.jpg
  galbitang.jpg

(These will be wired into the vocabulary cards in the next update.)

TIPS:
- For member portraits: square photos (1:1) work best. 600x600 px is plenty.
- For food: any clean composition. 800x800 px is fine.
- PNG keeps transparency. JPG is smaller but no transparency.
