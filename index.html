Lesson 6 at a glance

Order food the Korean way

In this lesson, you'll learn how to ask "How much is it?" (얼마예요?), order food using proper counting units, and have a natural conversation at a Korean restaurant. Perfect for beginners ready to use real Korean!

  • ① 9 essential Korean food words
  • ② Native Korean numbers 1–99
  • ③ 6 counting units for everyday use
  • ④ Real restaurant dialogue practice
A

비빔밥 한 그릇 주세요.

One bowl of bibimbap, please.

B

네, 잠깐만 기다리세요.

Sure, please wait a moment.
