📹 HOW TO ADD YOUR VIDEOS
===========================

Drop your video files into this folder. The website will play them automatically.

Use these exact filenames so the site finds them:

  member1.mp4   →  shown on Vocabulary page, Member 1 card
  member2.mp4   →  shown on Vocabulary page, Member 2 card
  member3.mp4   →  shown on Vocabulary page, Member 3 card
  member4.mp4   →  shown on Vocabulary page, Member 4 card
  member5.mp4   →  shown on Vocabulary page, Member 5 card
  dialogue.mp4  →  shown on Dialogue page (group role-play, 1–3 minutes)

TIPS:
- MP4 (H.264) works in every browser. Avoid MOV or AVI.
- Keep each vocab video under ~50 MB so the page loads quickly.
- For the dialogue: 1–3 minutes, show your face and voice (per coursework rules).
- NO AI voices — coursework gives 0 marks for AI-generated audio.

If you want different filenames, edit the src="..." attribute in:
  - vocabulary.html (search for "member1.mp4", etc.)
  - dialogue.html   (search for "dialogue.mp4")
