Our website

Korean for Beginners

Welcome! We are Group 4 from FOM 1, and this website is our project for the LMPU3282 — Korean for Beginners course.

Our goal is to make learning Korean fun, friendly, and approachable. We focused on Lesson 6: 얼마예요? (How much is it?) — teaching you how to order food at a Korean restaurant using the right vocabulary, counting units, and dialogue. Whether you're a total beginner or brushing up on the basics, we hope this site helps you take your first delicious step into the Korean language! 🍱
